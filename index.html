Chips

Chips are a common snack. They are found at most stores.

What are the different kinds?

There are many different kinds, but here are a few. There are potato chips, tortilla chips, and corn chips.

Who makes the chips?

The biggest chip-making company is Frito Lay. Frito Lay is primarily known for Lay's potato chips, Doritos, Cheetos, Fritos, and Tostitos. They control 60% of the snack chip market in the United States.

Who invented potato chips?

Potato chips were invented accidentally by George Crum in 1853. He was working as a chef at a resteraunt and someone kept returning fries, claiming they weren't crisp enough. George Crum sliced them as thin as he could, fried them in hot oil and added salt.

Fun facts!

Americans consume about 1 billion pounds of potato chips each year.

There are over 200 varieties of Lay's potato chips.
